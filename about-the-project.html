Welcome to the Citizen Social Work project

Citizens practice social work in more ways than one.

  • Professionally and on purpose.
  • Licensed and certified.
  • Voluntarily. Court ordered.
  • Community based. Faith based.
  • In groups. With families.
  • At school. At church. In court.
  • For civil rights.
  • For food.
  • For housing.

As professional social workers, we choose to dedicate our working lives to social work practice and in return we recieve training comradory to cope with stresses of practice.

In addition to professional social work

Many doctors, lawyers, teachers, even business executives cross into the ambiguous boundaries of social work practice when patients, clients and students can't afford medical costs, legal fees or breakfast.

In addition to helping professionals

Still, helping professionals are not alone praciticng social work. Parents, friends, neighbors, strangers help each other navigate frustrating healthcare systems.


Citizen Social Work

Space to promote evidence-based best-practices in social work to help more citizens help more citizens resonsibly.

Becuase every citizen practicing social work –intentionally or not, full-time and on the side– deserves to know the best way to help.
